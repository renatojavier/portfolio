Project Overview

The client specified the requirements and gave the mockup from Photoshop. He wanted an impressive introductory presentation for the company UKBug Plus (his client I think).

I applied svg path animation on correct sequence. After the animation it will send a request to the server-side (which was out from my scope of work from this project) then populate all fetched data on the lavender section below the animated texts.

  • Project manager: Tariq Kamal
  • Skills applied: json, svg, jquery, javascript, greensock-gsap, css3, html5, photoshop
  • Completion date: October 2015
  • Accessibility: Prototype

This was a project from UK Dev Plus, but this copy was a prototype which I have all the right to upload on my server.
